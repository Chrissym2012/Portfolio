Golden Snitch Shape Project

This project was inspired by the Golden Snitch from Harry Potter and focuses on using shape to create visual balance and movement. I incorporated several Gestalt principles into the design — similarity through repeating circular forms, continuity through the fluid motion of the wings, and closure by allowing the viewer’s eye to complete the outline of the Snitch.

These principles help guide the viewer’s eye across the composition and create a cohesive, harmonious visual that captures the energy and elegance of the Snitch in flight.
